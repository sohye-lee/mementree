        memoir field / 00 — empty

        an empty field.

        before anything else, plant the first tree.
        the kind of tree you choose shapes what this field becomes.

        [ 01 ] what kind of tree?
        • ◐
          a project
          things you build with others. each tree is one. memos are milestones, decisions, what the team learned.
        • ✦
          a wish
          in the spirit of yoko ono's wish trees. plant one and tie wishes — yours, or anyone you invite.
        • ●
          a diary
          one tree per month. each day a memo. walk back through the year by walking the field.
        • ○
          a note
          freeform. trees are topics, memos are scraps. for thinking, reading, collecting.
        [ 02 ] name the first tree
        a short, low-key name. you can rename later.
        a brief, in one breath — what is this tree about.
        you can plant more whenever. the field grows with you.
